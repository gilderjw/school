Activity Description:

Two users were asked to complete a series of tasks in Google Chrome. The tasks were as listed below:

1. Search for new webpages

2. Return to a bookmarked/saved page

3. Return to a particular non-bookmarked page

Executive Summary

High level overview of the activity:

Two users were asked to complete a series of tasks in Google Chrome. The first user, Cary Laxer, was unfamiliar with Chrome. The second user, Chad Estill, was familiar with it. By timing each user as they attempted to complete the task, we are able to gather quantitative data regarding how their familiarity affects their usage of the software.
